)

Defined in src/davinci-eight/math/Matrix4.ts:117

Sets the elements of the target matrix to the perspective transformation. The perspective transformation maps homogeneous world coordinates into a cubic viewing volume such that an orthogonal projection of that viewing volume will give the correct linear perspective.

Parameters:

  • fov Number

    field of view in the vertical direction, measured in radians.

  • aspect Number

    The ratio of view width divided by view height.

  • near Number

    The distance to the near field plane.

  • far Number

    The distance to the far field plane.
